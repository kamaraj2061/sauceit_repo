Privacy Policy — Sauceit Industrial Kitchen Suite

Last updated: 14 Nov 2025

Sauceit is a SaaS-based industrial kitchen management platform developed and fully operated in association with Foodswing. This Privacy Policy explains how Sauceit and Foodswing (“we”, “our”, “us”) collect, use, store, and protect user information.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • User ID
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Access role and permissions

b. Operational & Production Data:

  • Recipe and production data
  • Yield and quantity details
  • Inventory usage and stock-in/out logs
  • Order counts and planning data
  • Kitchen workflow logs

c. Device & Technical Information:

  • Device identifiers
  • App activity logs
  • Crash logs
  • Login timestamps

d. Location Data (only if logistics tracking is enabled):

  • GPS tracking for routing, dispatch, and vehicle monitoring

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and maintain the Sauceit platform
  • Manage production, planning, and inventory
  • Track kitchen operations and logistics
  • Generate dashboards, insights, and audit trails
  • Improve security, reliability, and system performance

We do NOT use or share your data for advertising or marketing.

3. Information Sharing

Information may be shared only with:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • ERP / weighing scale integrations
  • Google Maps API

We never sell or rent personal information.

4. Data Security

Your data is protected with encryption and controlled access mechanisms.
